1. During a semester, a lecturer reads one or more lectures.
2. Sometimes the lecturer is on leave to focus on doing research; in this case, (s)he does not give a lecture.
3. A student usually attends one or more lectures, unless (s)he has something better to do.
4. During the semester, there will be several exercises which are meant to be solved by small study groups.
5. Each student is assigned to one particular study group for the whole semester in the course management system.
6. A study group consists of two to three students.
7. After submission of a solution by a study group, it is graded by a tutor.
