Cafe Management System:
The system must allow users to log in with existing credentials or create a new account for access.
The system must provide a centralized dashboard where users can view key metrics, notifications, and navigate to other functionalities.
The system must enable users to add, update, and delete items in the inventory, track stock levels, set reorder points, and automate restocking processes.
The system must facilitate the creation, modification, and deletion of menu items, allowing customization of menu categories and pricing. Menu images should also be available.
The system must streamline the order placement process for customers, providing order confirmation and status updates to both customers and staff.
The system must maintain a database of customer profiles, capturing and storing customer preferences and order history.
The system must generate comprehensive graphs (such as Area Chart and Bar Chart) on sales and customer trends. Jasper reports must be used to produce receipts for customers.
