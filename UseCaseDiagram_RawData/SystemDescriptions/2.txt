eDoc facilitates the input and organization of medical records, covering past diagnoses, surgeries, allergies,
medications, immunizations, and family medical histories. eDoc incorporates a robust appointment
scheduling system and secure data storage compliant with healthcare data protection regulations. Users can
efficiently search and filter patient records, add detailed notes, manage medications with reminders, and store
lab results, imaging, and diagnostic reports. Telemedicine integration enables remote patient monitoring and
virtual visits. Patients can access their records via secure portals, and healthcare providers can analyze patient
data trends and collaborate on cases. The app ensures data security. Regular backups and data recovery
options are in place to prevent data loss.
