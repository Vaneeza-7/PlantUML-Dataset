Clinic Management System:

1. The system must allow for patient registration and management of patient information, including personal details, medical history, and contact information.

2. The system must support appointment scheduling, allowing patients to book appointments, and must send reminders to both patients and staff.

3. The system must facilitate billing and invoicing, generating accurate bills based on services provided and tracking payment statuses.

4. The system must enable staff collaboration and communication, allowing healthcare professionals to share information and communicate seamlessly within the system.

5. The system must generate reports and analytics based on patient data, appointments, and billing information, providing insights for operational improvement.
