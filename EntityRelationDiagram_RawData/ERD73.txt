The Budget Travel Planner app is designed to assist users in planning affordable trips while ensuring they stay within their financial limits. With the rising costs of travel, this app provides essential tools and resources to help travelers maximize their experiences without overspending. By combining budget management with travel planning, users can explore new destinations and enjoy memorable experiences while keeping their finances in check.

A standout feature of the app is the Itinerary Building tool, which allows users to create detailed travel plans by organizing their travel dates, destinations, and activities. Users can input their desired budget for each segment of their trip, and the app will offer personalized recommendations for low-cost accommodations and activities that fit within their financial constraints. This tailored approach helps users discover affordable options they might not have considered, ensuring they can enjoy their travels without breaking the bank.

The Budget Tracking feature enables users to log expenses throughout their trip, providing real-time insights into their spending habits. Users can categorize their expenses—such as lodging, food, transportation, and activities—to see where their money is going. This functionality helps travelers make informed decisions on the fly, allowing them to adjust their spending to stay within their planned budget. Additionally, the Trip History section allows users to save previous itineraries and budget logs, enabling them to learn from past trips and apply successful strategies to future travels.

To further enhance the user experience, the app includes a Currency Converter, which provides real-time exchange rates and allows users to easily convert their budget into the local currency of their travel destination. This feature helps travelers navigate foreign expenses more comfortably. Overall, the Budget Travel Planner app empowers users to embark on exciting adventures while effectively managing their finances, ensuring that every traveler can create unforgettable memories without the stress of overspending.
