The ‘Textile Management System’ case study is designed to monitor all the processes in a textile manufacturing industry. There are various processes such as order receiving, purchase of raw 
materials, classification of raw materials, production of goods and selling the products. The main 
aim of this system is to ensure the reliability of data and to ease the process of manufacturing and 
transportation of garments and raw materials. This system ensures that the hardships prevailing 
in the manual systems are overridden by reducing the errors in data handling. The major functionalities are customer registration, sampling, stock checking, purchasing and processing of raw 
materials, production of garments, product checking and clearance, shipment, billing and report 
generation
