The HealthCare Management System has these functions:
The Creation and Management of Patient Profiles. Comprehensive Medical History Input and Organization. Advanced Appointment Scheduling with Reminders. Secure Data Storage Compliant with Healthcare Regulations Integration of Telemedicine Practices. Patient Access via Secure Portals. Data Analysis, Collaboration, and Reporting for Healthcare Teams. Import/Export Capabilities for Data Accessibility. Regular Backups and Data Recovery Options. Medication Management with Reminders. Record Storage for Lab Results, Imaging, and Diagnostic Report.
